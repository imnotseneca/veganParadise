About us:

How we made this Vegan Paradise:


After questioning Specism, Bruno, the owner of Vegan Paradise, made a huge change in his life knowing it will help other sentient beings, the planet Earth, and eventually his whole health. He started to experiment mixing different kind of plant-based food to create tasty, nourishing and good looking plates that doesn't involve animal suffering. In 2020, Bruno and other small group of people invest on Vegan Paradise to bring some happy moments to the public, showing you can eat tasty and nourishing food without involving animals corpses or their secretions.
